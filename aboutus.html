A Little Bit About Us

On The Edge is a family-owned coffee shop in Old Town Clovis, California, founded by Scott and Julie Glenn.

We offer carefully sourced and roasted coffee beans, providing a rich and flavorful blend for coffee enthusiasts.

Our menu features delicious food items made from fresh ingredients, including bagel sandwiches, salads, and pastries.

In addition to our delightful offerings, we also host live music events, adding a touch of entertainment and creating a vibrant atmosphere for our customers.
