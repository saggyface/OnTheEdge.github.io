Start Your Day Right with
On The Edge's Delicious Coffee

Life is short, and that's why we should cherish the simple pleasures that make every day worthwhile. As the famous quote goes, 'life is short, enjoy your coffee.' At OTE, we believe that a good cup of coffee can brighten up your day and give you the energy to tackle any challenge. That's why we're passionate about serving you the finest coffee blends and providing you with a cozy and welcoming atmosphere. So come in, take a seat, and let us help you savor every moment.

Join Us for a Night of Music!

Unwind to the sound of live music every Friday and Saturday at On The Edge. Our local musicians will serenade you while you enjoy our wine and delectable treats. Join us for a laid-back evening you won't forget.

Come Discover a World of Flavor!

At OTE, we believe that great coffee should be accompanied by equally delicious food. That's why we offer a diverse menu of mouth-watering treats that are perfect for any time of the day. From savory bagel sandwiches to scrumptious mini pizzas, our food items are made with fresh ingredients and bursting with flavor. Come and indulge in our wraps, bagels, and sandwiches that are sure to satisfy your cravings!
